# Guided coach audio notice

These are synthetic, fixed workout cues generated with
[Kokoro-82M](https://huggingface.co/hexgrad/Kokoro-82M) and the
[Kokoro inference library](https://github.com/hexgrad/kokoro), both released
under Apache-2.0. The source voice identifiers and exact generation settings
are recorded in `scripts/generate-coach-audio.py`.

The packs contain no user, health, exercise, or personal-trainer data. The
coach names are product labels for synthetic voices; they are not real people.
